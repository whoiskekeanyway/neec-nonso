toilet in,toilet out.

toilet in, toilet out is a project that re-enacts the dual worlds we live in the toilet; the escapades and the escapes in this little room of 'relief'. shiting is a religion, and it unfolds not only in the toilet, but also in the mind. in the inside of the mind are repressed emotions; buried ideas, feelings and deep desires waiting to implode, as we offload the shits living inside us. this innate things sometimes escape us, as we rebel and express them raw; vandalising the four gestapo like walls of the toilet with graffiti.
