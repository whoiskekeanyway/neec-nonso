the another beginning

the another beginning is a prophecyâ€”a visual exploration of a world reimagined in the aftermath of the novel virus, covid-19. through photography, i illuminate my vision of a possible future: what if the virus wins the war?

this series was born during the height of the pandemic, a time when fear gripped me as i witnessed the fragility of our ability to contain the outbreak. i questioned our survival and imagined a world where only a few adams and eves remain, tasked with rebuilding humanity from the ashes of a global collapse. in this imagined future, new beginnings emerge, along with new ways of living and connecting.

the another beginning reflects on the resilience of the human spirit. it is a meditation on survival, healing, and the deep human instinct to rebuild and thrive against all odds. in this work, i explore not just the uncertainty and vulnerability of those dark moments but also the enduring hope and strength that define what it means to be human.
